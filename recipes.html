Formulas & Ratios

Standard formulations for daily poultry management.

Chick Water Formula

Daily drinking water supplementation

4 ml LABs
+
13 ml FPJ
+
19 ml Vinegar
+
133 ml Sea Water

Per gallon of fresh water. Alternative: 3g sea salt instead of sea water.

Calcium Supplement

Separate from daily water - for layers

4 ml WCA per gallon

Provide separately from regular chick water. Essential for laying hens.

Fermented Feed Ratio

Weight-based formula

6 lbs Feed
:
8.34 lbs Water

1:1.39 weight ratio. Add 4ml LAB + 8ml FPJ per gallon of water used.

Fermented Sea Water (Chicken Health Variant)

Post-molting recovery & eggshell strengthening

126 ml Sea Water
+
19 ml Vinegar
+
8 ml WCA

Per gallon of fresh water. Feed to flock once every two days to promote health after molting or to thicken eggshells.

Coop Bedding Spray Solution

Deep litter maintenance

4 ml LAB
+
8 ml FPJ
+
126 ml Seawater
+
8 ml Vinegar

For Initial Setup: Spray the coop bedding (chopped straw/wood chips/wood shavings) heavily with this mixture when first establishing the floor.

For Corrective Action: If the bedding becomes too wet or begins to emit a foul odor, use a 4ml LAB per gallon solution to spot-check those areas, as the organic acids will sterilize and suppress ammonia-producing pathogens.
